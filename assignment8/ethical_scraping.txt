Task 5:

1.) Which sections of the website are restricted for crawling?
The main restricted sections are the lines with Disallow: under User-agent: *.  
The broadest restricted areas are:
/w/ 
/api/ 
/trap/
/wiki/Special

2.)  Are there specific rules for certain user agents?
Yes.  Some bots are fully blocked while others have crawl delays.

3.)  Why use robots.txt?
Allow website owners control over how automated agents interact with their sites.  Prevents overloading server,
protects sensitive or irrelevant content from being indexed, and sets clear expectations for crawlers.
By following it, scrapers behave ethically and reduce the risk of legal issues, service disruption, or accessing
data the site owner has explicitly chosen to keep private.
